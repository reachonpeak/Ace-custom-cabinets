About Us

Where precision meets passion

Our Story

Building Dreams
One Cabinet at a Time

ACE Custom Cabinets was founded with a simple mission: to bring exceptional craftsmanship and design to every home and business we serve. Based in Edmonton, Alberta, we've built our reputation on a commitment to quality that goes beyond expectations.

Our team of skilled artisans combines time-honored woodworking techniques with modern design technology to create cabinetry that is both beautiful and enduring. Every piece that leaves our workshop reflects the care, precision, and passion we pour into our work.

We don't just build cabinets — we craft living environments that enhance the way you live, work, and entertain. From initial consultation to final installation, we are with you every step of the way.
